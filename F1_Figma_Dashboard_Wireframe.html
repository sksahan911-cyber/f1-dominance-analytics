Racing Eras
How dominant teams shifted across 75 seasons of Formula 1
Era Timeline
1950 — 1959
The Pioneer Era
Alfa Romeo and Ferrari battled for supremacy in F1's founding decade. Juan Manuel Fangio won 5 world championships, a record that stood for 46 years.
Alfa RomeoFerrariMaseratiJuan Manuel Fangio
1960 — 1979
The Technical Revolution
Lotus under Colin Chapman introduced the monocoque chassis and rear-engine design. Jim Clark and Jackie Stewart defined this era of innovation and tragedy.
LotusTyrrellJim ClarkJackie Stewart
1980 — 1999
The Turbo & Glory Years
The greatest rivalry in motorsport: Senna vs Prost. McLaren-Honda dominated with turbo power. Williams took over in the 1990s with Mansell and Damon Hill.
McLarenWilliamsAyrton SennaAlain Prost
2000 — 2013
The Schumacher & Vettel Dynasties
Schumacher's Ferrari dominated 2000–2004 with 5 consecutive titles. Then Vettel and Red Bull took 4 straight championships 2010–2013.
FerrariRed BullSchumacherVettel
2014 — 2021
The Mercedes Silver Arrows Era
Unprecedented dominance. Mercedes won 8 consecutive constructors' championships. Lewis Hamilton surpassed Schumacher's all-time win record of 91 victories.
MercedesLewis HamiltonNico Rosberg8 Titles
2022 — Present
The New Guard
New regulations in 2022 reset the grid. Red Bull and Verstappen dominated 2022–2024. McLaren emerged as 2025 front-runners with Lando Norris leading.
Red BullMcLarenVerstappenNorris
